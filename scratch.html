Stargazing Tips

  • Always go to a dark place, away from city lights.
  • Use a red flashlight to protect your night vision.
  • Check the weather forecast for clear skies.
  • Take a telescope or binoculars for a closer view of stars and planets.
  • Be patient and enjoy the beauty of the night sky!
